# Faytuks Network

> OSINT network covering breaking geopolitical events, military developments & global news in real time.

---

## What we do

We aggregate and verify breaking news from around the world using open-source intelligence (OSINT) techniques — satellite imagery, local sources, official statements, and on-the-ground reports. Our contributors and affiliates monitor developing situations 24/7 across conflict zones, diplomatic flashpoints, and major world events.
